About Me

I'm a UX Designer with 5+ years of experience designing enterprise-scale digital products across healthcare, travel, and EdTech. Currently with GE Healthcare via Tata Consultancy Services, I enjoy simplifying dense, workflow-heavy products — multi-team approval chains, fragmented data sources, dashboards trying to do too much — into interfaces that feel obvious instead of intimidating.

I believe in simplifying complexity, not decorating it. A deep focus on usability and stakeholder workflows — not just visual polish — is what makes an enterprise product actually get adopted.
