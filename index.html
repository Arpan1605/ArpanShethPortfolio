Hi! I am Arpan Sheth

I’m a self-taught UX/UI designer from Gandhinagar who got into design by noticing how every product decision changes the way people feel while using an app.

As I explored different design disciplines, I kept coming back to user experience: structure, hierarchy, flow, friction, and the tiny moments that make an interface feel obvious instead of confusing.

I now enjoy work that sits between practical usability and visual sharpness, especially when I can simplify complexity, shape a stronger narrative, and make digital products feel more trustworthy.
