About Me

I'm a UX/UI Designer with 5+ years of experience designing web applications end-to-end across enterprise, startup, and agency-style freelance engagements in healthcare, travel, and EdTech. Currently with GE Healthcare via Tata Consultancy Services, I pair UX thinking with hands-on frontend development in Angular and Git/GitHub to close the gap between design intent and what actually ships — simplifying dense, workflow-heavy products into interfaces that feel obvious instead of intimidating.

My design approach

I believe in simplifying complexity, not decorating it. A deep focus on usability and stakeholder workflows — not just visual polish — is what makes an enterprise product actually get adopted.

I'm comfortable adapting design decisions to varied regional and market contexts — from a US-based travel booking platform to India-based hospitality operations — and I'm currently learning German (A1, working toward B1 by August 2026), open to relocating anywhere in Germany for enterprise UX roles.
